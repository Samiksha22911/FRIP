# 📌 Overview

**Faculty Performance Tracker (FRIP – Faculty Research Impact Portal)** is a web-based system designed to **monitor, manage, and evaluate faculty performance** at the college level.

The platform tracks academic achievements such as:

* Research papers
* Book publications
* Conference papers
* Journal papers

It also enables **students to explore research work and collaborate with faculty**, making it a complete academic ecosystem.

# 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to provide a **transparent, structured, and data-driven system** to evaluate faculty performance based on measurable academic metrics while also **encouraging student–faculty research collaboration**.

# ✨ Features

### 👨‍💼 Admin

* Add and manage faculty, HOD, and student accounts
* View all research data (papers, books, conferences)
* Monitor overall faculty performance
* Manage system data and analytics

### 🏫 HOD (Head of Department)

* View department-wise faculty details
* Monitor research contributions of faculty
* Track performance within the department

### 👨‍🏫 Faculty

* Submit research papers, book publications, and conference details
* Manage and update their academic contributions
* View personal performance dashboard

### 🎓 Student

* Explore all research papers and publications
* Search and filter faculty based on research interests
* Select faculty for research guidance or collaboration
* View academic contributions across departments

# 📊 Key Functionalities

* 📄 Research paper & publication tracking
* 📚 Academic contribution management
* 📈 Performance evaluation system
* 🏆 Faculty ranking based on achievements
* 📊 Interactive dashboard for insights
* 🔍 Research exploration for students
